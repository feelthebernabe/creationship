the principles

how the room agrees to work

the creationship runs on trust. these are the few things we ask everyone to be upfront about, so the room stays a place people want to keep showing up to.

ethics and clarity around financial exchange

if you're proposing a project for the room to build, please be upfront about whether it's:

  • purely altruistic — a gift for everyone
  • part of an existing for-profit or nonprofit
  • an idea that might become a new initiative or company
  • a project that supports your personal brand or business

all of these are fine. clarity is what we ask for.

if a project becomes financially generative, those who helped ideate and iterate should share in the success — proportional to what they put in.

consent and credit

the room runs on shared half-formed thoughts. that only works if people trust how their ideas get used.

  • if you want to build on someone's idea, ask them
  • if you ship something, credit the people who shaped it — including the early conversations
  • if someone asks not to be named in a write-up, photo, or post, that holds

the simplest version: ask first, credit generously.

this is a living document. the room evolves and so do the things we need to name out loud. bring tensions, edge cases, and revisions to a sunday.
