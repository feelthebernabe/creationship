the creationship

a weekly sunday gathering for people with moral ambition. an infinite hackathon — a place to build things that matter, together. there are three ways to be part of it.

hold the space

arrive early, stay present, hold the container so others can build inside it. every hackathon needs someone keeping the room alive.

teach

bring an offering — a skill, a framework, a practice — and share it with the room. this is the sprint briefing.

brain trust

lend your thinking, your questions, your honesty — be part of the collective intelligence that never stops shipping.
